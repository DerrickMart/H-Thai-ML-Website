Origin Story

It all started with the sweet aroma of home cooked Thai food growing up. Our team here at H-Thai-ML would not be where we are today without our founder, Amara. Being a mother and wife of a loving family, Amara has been cooking delicious meals for the last 5 decades. After her children left the nest, she decided to put her skills to the test. Amara opened our work families' first resurant on April 4, 1994 in Raleigh, NC. Feeding the local community in Raleigh, her work family started to rapidly expand. Every community we have served has brought nothing but delight and satisfaction to each customer.
